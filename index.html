Hi, I’m a Computer Science graduate with a passion for creating and deploying innovative digital solutions.

My tech journey began in 2022 as a curious front-end engineer determined to build my personal website — a place to showcase the projects I created on my PC. After months of learning and coding, I finally developed my first site, but faced a new challenge: hosting it.

Research led me to AWS S3. Following tutorials, I successfully hosted my website and was instantly drawn to the simplicity of running applications without managing my own server. That moment sparked my deep interest in cloud technologies.

Since then, I have earned AWS certifications, built multiple real-world projects, and spent countless days (and nights!) studying, experimenting, and mastering the AWS ecosystem. My experience spans building, managing, and deploying scalable, real-time projects — and I’m always eager to push the boundaries of what’s possible in the cloud.
